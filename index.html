The Western Cape Policing Department is part of the South African Police Services. We as the Western Cape Government promotes the sharing of data among our police stations. This is an important role to monitor crime in the city. This monitoring of crime is expanding on the 2019-2024 Western Cape strategic plan for fighting crime in the province. The crime statistical data shared is for all plolice stations within the province. The future crime statistics to be shared in this website based on International Classification of Crimefor Statistical Purposes(ICCS) as a national standard used in South Africa as from 2016. ICCS was originated by UNITED NATIONS OFFICE ON DRUGS AND CRIME (UNODC).
